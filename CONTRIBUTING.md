# Contributing to Afghanistan Tours

Thank you for considering contributing! We welcome:

- Improvements to our documentation
- Suggestions for new tours or destinations
- Security and safety tips for travelers

## Guidelines

- Be respectful and constructive in your contributions.
- When suggesting travel info, please provide reliable sources or firsthand experience.
- For code-related contributions, open an issue to discuss your plan before submitting a pull request.

**Learn more about us at https://www.afghanistantours.af/.**
