Travel tips & safety highlights

Always check the latest travel advisories and entry requirements.
Respect local customs: dress modestly in public spaces; ask before photographing people.
Health: bring personal medications, basic first-aid kit; consult your doctor about vaccinations.
Currency & payments: Afghan Afghani (AFN) is commonly used; major card acceptance is limited in remote areas.
